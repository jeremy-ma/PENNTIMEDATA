Student Record System (SRS)

Billing System (SFSEASI)

  • Composite Inquiry (CIQ)
    Online application used by university departments to view student billing account information.

  • Student Account Adjustment (SAJ)
    Online application used by pre authorized university departments to process adjustments to student billing accounts.


Student Aid Management System (SAM)

  • Student Aid Management System (SAM) / Departmental Grant Processing
    Online application used by pre authorized university departments to process departmental grants (e.g. Research Assistantship, Research Fellowship, Teaching Assistantship and Departmental Grants).

  • To schedule training for SFSEASI and SAM contact Angela Caporelli, Customer Support Representative for Student Registration and Financial Services, 215-898-2337, caporelli@sfs.upenn.edu.  Dates and times for these sessions are list on SFSEASI and SAM training calendar. 


SFSEASI and SAM training calendar


Authorized Forms Required for SFSEASI / SAM Training

  • SFSEASI Security Application
    (required for SFSEASI and SAM)

  • SRFS Privacy Statement for SFSEASI and SAM / HIPPA
    (required for SFSEASI and SAM)

  • Confidentiality of Student Records Access to SFSEASI and SAM
    (required for SFSEASI and SAM)

  • ISC User Account Logon Form
    (required for SFSEASI and SAM)

  • Student Registration and Financial Services Approval Form
    (required for SAJ System Access Only)
